#### Battle

+ We head down the lab's second level, looking for the queen.
+ 0) Super seize.  Julie takes lead into the grey antechamber, dropping two.  Jean and Billie dives in for more.  Chirp, R9, and Gizmo move up to the entrance.
+ 1) Captain slows down a praetorian, and Jean softens up a couple.  Billie cleaves through a wounded one to kill another cowering in the corner. All the glyphids close in on Jean, who luckily fends them off all 6, leaving a couple of them dripping ichor. Gizmo tosses a stun grenade on to the pile. R9 taps out one and draws off the praetorian. Chirp drops another. 
+ 2) Gizmo tosses another grenade while Chirp murders another one, and Billie kills the rest. 
+ 3) After a pause to regroup, Julie leads the charge again, blasting at the queen. Gizmo tosses a frak grenade, damaging the queen and the hive. R9 blasts at a spitter. Billie goes for the queen. Chirp coasts in behind the group. Jean takes out the spitter and wounds a slasher. 
+ 4) Julie lays into the queen, and Gizmo tosses a grenade. R9 softens up a spitter. Billie cleaves into some stuff. The queen comes for Billie! And a spine tinks off of R9.  The pack mobs Billie. Jean takes the armor off of the queen and Chirp hurts her.
+ 5) Jean wounds the queen, and Julie takes her down! Billie runs from one swarm to another. Chirp runs for higher ground, snapping at the hive. A praetorian comes for the queen. A spitter misses R9.  A new pack comes for Billie, who is raging out and manages to slaughter them. Gizmo tosses another grenade on the hive, with little effect. R9 taks out a spitter and shoots into the hive.  Two more packs stream out of the hive!
+ 6) Chirp scrambles up onto high ground but fails to drop a spitter while Julie shoots.  Billie takes one out and smacks into a praetorian. Chirp deflects a spit. Billie rages as the spawns pile onto him! Jean and R9 fire into the hive. Gizmo launches another grenade into the hive and hits a spitter. 
+ 7) R9 takes out the hive and a spitter! Billie drops a couple while Gizmo drops a stun grenade on those around him. Jean and Julie work on the swarm behind them.  The remainder piles onto Billie, and most fall to the ground. A couple come for Jean, and she fumbles into a kill. Cloud of spores comes out!
